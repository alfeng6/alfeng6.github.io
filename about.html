Hi! I'm Albert, a Junior studying Computer Science at Stanford University passionate about creating products and experiences with user-centered design. Most recently I've finished an amazing summer working as a product management intern at Tableau Software, where I was able to learn how to start from user goals and build seemless user experiences and beautiful features. My interest in human-computer interaction stems from a belief that because most products have humans as the end user, we ought to create products that are designed in a way that is intuitive and delightful to use for people.
